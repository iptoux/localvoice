# Getting Started

## What It Does

LocalVoice is a small floating overlay that lets you dictate text into any application on your desktop — entirely offline, using a local Whisper model.

## First Launch

When you start LocalVoice for the first time:

1. A small **pill** appears in the top-left corner of your screen.
2. The pill shows **"Ready"** when the app is idle.
3. A tray icon appears in the system notification area.

## The Pill

The pill is the primary interface. It is always on top and stays out of your way.

| State | Colour | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Ready | Dark grey | Idle, waiting for input |
| Listening… | Red | Recording your voice |
| Transcribing… | Amber | Processing audio |
| Done | Green | Text delivered |
| Error | Rose | Something went wrong |

**Drag** the pill anywhere on screen by clicking and holding it.
**Double-click** the pill to open the full LocalVoice window.

## The Full Window

Open the full window from:

- Double-clicking the pill
- Clicking **Open App** in the tray menu

The full window gives you access to Dashboard, History, Dictionary, Models, and Settings.

## Tray Icon

Right-click the tray icon for quick actions: Open App, Settings, Quit.
Left-click the tray icon to show or hide the pill.
